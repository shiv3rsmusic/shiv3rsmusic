About me

SHIV3RS is a House music specialist based in Nottingham, UK. With experience mixing a range of styles including French Rap, Reggaeton, RnB, Drum & Bass and Bollywood, SHIV3RS is a versatile artist who has performed in various bars and clubs in Gibraltar and Nottingham. Their talent has taken them overseas, where they have performed in Montpellier, France. Known for their ability to read the crowd and keep the energy high, SHIV3RS's sets are guaranteed to get the dance floor moving. With a passion for music that shines through in every performance, SHIV3RS is a rising star in the UK music scene.
